Insta Web Text Guide

  1. Download [Kiwi Browser](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.kiwibrowser.browser) from the Play Store.
  2. Open Kiwi Browser, click on the three dots in the top-right corner.
  3. Click on the extension options (given at number 7).
  4. Click on the "+ (from store)" option.
  5. Now search for "J2TEAM Cookies."
  6. Click on the "Add to Chrome" button.
  7. Give permission by clicking on the OK button.
  8. Open a new tab in Kiwi Browser, and search for "instagram.com."
  9. Login to Instagram with the desired account.
  10. After successfully logging in, click on the three dots again.
  11. Scroll down and search for "J2TEAM Cookies" (You will find it at the end).
  12. Click on it.
  13. Now you will see an Export button, with a down arrow. Click on the dropdown arrow.
  14. Click on "Export as text."
  15. The "Insta Web Text" is now successfully copied to your clipboard. You just have to paste it now!
